About Our School

Established in 1995, Nepal Police School in Garuda, Rautahat has been a beacon of quality education in the region. Our mission is to provide holistic education that nurtures intellectual, physical, emotional, and social growth.

We offer education from Nursery to Grade 10 with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that balances academics with extracurricular activities.

Our campus spans over 5 acres with state-of-the-art classrooms, science labs, computer lab, library, sports facilities, and a peaceful learning environment.
